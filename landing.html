● Questions

Things teachers always ask

Does it really work offline?▾
Yes. The whole app shell, your boards, and student data are cached locally. Plan a lesson on the subway, run a class with weak Wi-Fi, sync everything the moment you're back online.
Why a PWA instead of an App Store app?▾
Same install experience for the user, but we ship updates instantly without Apple/Google review. Install is 3 MB instead of 200, and one codebase serves iPhone, Android, and laptop.
How do I install on iPhone?▾
Open this page in Safari (Apple restricts PWA install to Safari only). Tap the Share icon, then "Add to Home Screen." TeachEd will appear as a regular app, no browser bar.
What languages does it support?▾
English is the primary supported language with a full game library covering grammar (articles, prepositions, phrasal verbs, conditionals, tenses) and vocabulary from A1 to C2. The interface is English; boards work for any language.
Can students use it too?▾
Yes. Students get a dedicated portal showing their boards, progress, vocab, and games. They install the same PWA and sign in with the email you invite them with.
Is my data secure?▾
All traffic is HTTPS. Passwords are bcrypt-hashed. Sessions use signed JWTs with 7-day expiry. Backend runs on Render with PostgreSQL. No student data is ever sold or shared.
Can I cancel anytime?▾
Yes — one tap in the billing portal, no questions. Your boards and student data stay accessible on the Free tier after downgrade.
