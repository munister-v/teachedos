FAQ

Common questions

Can I really use it for free forever?▾
Yes. The Free plan never expires. You get 3 boards and 5 students per board — enough for individual tutors starting out. Upgrade when you need more.
Does TeachedOS work offline?▾
Yes. Every change saves to your browser's localStorage immediately. If you lose internet, you keep working. When you're back online, changes sync to the cloud automatically.
How does the Zoom / Google Meet integration work?▾
You save your permanent room URLs in the Calls window. When you click "Go Live" on a schedule slot, all students enrolled in that class see a real-time "🔴 Live Now" banner with a direct join link. No plugins required.
What languages and levels does the assignment builder support?▾
The built-in task library covers English grammar topics (articles, prepositions, phrasal verbs, conditionals, tenses, and more) from A1 to C2. Gap-fill, MCQ, true/false, and matching question types are all included — no internet or AI API needed.
Can students use TeachedOS on mobile?▾
Yes. The student dashboard is fully mobile-responsive. The board editor works on tablets and larger phones. The macOS desktop interface (teacher view) is optimized for laptop/desktop screens.
Is my data secure?▾
All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Sessions use signed JWTs with 7-day expiry. The backend runs on Render with PostgreSQL — no student data is ever sold or shared.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?▾
Yes, cancel anytime from the billing portal — no questions asked. Your boards and student data remain accessible on the Free plan after downgrade.
